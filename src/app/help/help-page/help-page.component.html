Adding a product

Go to side menu and tap on Add Item. If you can't access the side menu, either you're using a smaller screen device or you have minimized it. In that case, click to toggle the side menu.

By taping on the Add Item menu option, you'll navigate to Add Item page. The page has 3 sections that you'll have to fill inorder to add your product to the store.

Product Details Section
This section contains input for all the basic information about a product. The product name, price, available stock amount, color information ...
Original Manufacturer input isn't required if you're the product manufacturer. You only have to fill that if the product is imported or if you're distributing the product on behalf of another seller
Product Image Section
In this section you have to upload your product images. It is very important that you take mulitple angles(faces) of your product as the discriptive image implies. That way customers can have a wholesome view of your product
3D model selection section
Every Product needs as 3D model to be part of your store. You can choose pre existing similar model or choose the option to request a custom model to be created for you by our team. More on that on 3D Model creation help part ...

Accessing products

Go to side menu and tap on Products. If you can't access the side menu, either you're using a smaller screen device or you have minimized it. In that case, click to toggle the side menu.

By taping on the Products menu option, you'll navigate to products page. All the products you've uploaded to store live here. Your products are organized using key words such as "Low on Stock" and "Best Sellers" for convenient access.

Alternatively, you can go to side menu and tap on Categories. There you can access your products organized based on categoires you've set when you added the products

Furthermore, you can search products by tapping on the search bar in either one of the pages.

Managing company details & stats

To access general and specific stats like sales stats navigate to account page by clicking Account. If you can't access the side menu, either you're using a smaller screen device or you have minimized it. In that case, click to toggle the side menu.

The first 2 sections display product sales upticks and various transaction related stats while the last section lists details of your company account.

To modify the company details listed on the product page, navigate to settings page by clicking on Settings from the side menu. In the first 2 sections of the settings page, you can modify the details of your company including account passowrd.

Accessing 3D Model catalog

Navigate to 3D Models page by clicking 3D Models

You can find all the models associated with your products in the page. You can browse and search the models within the page.

Deactivating your account

We hate to see you go but if you insist on leaving, avigate to settings page by clicking on Settings from the side menu.

In the Deactivate Your Account section, fill the reason for deactivating your account if you wish to and click on button. Your account will then be deleted.

This can't be reversed and all your information and products will be scrubbed from out database.

Accessing customer feedback

Customers can give you feedback on for your service or products. To view their messages, navigate to product detail page and click on Comments. you can interact and reply back to their comments.

Payment & Delivery
