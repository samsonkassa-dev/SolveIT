About Solve IT

iCog Labs incorporated some of it's previous project ideas and resources and came up with Solve IT; a nationwide innovation competition involving more than 15 cities in Ethiopia. Solve IT capitalizes the involvement of regional cities as a crucial factor in the creation of a sustainable local tech market. With such spirit, Solve IT was proposed to the US Embassy in Addis Ababa in late 2017. Now it is implemented with the US Embassy, JICA Ethiopia, and Humanity+.

This initiative aims to, primarily, decentralize technology and include the youth in problem-solving programs. Solve IT is also a marketplace where young innovators and investors connect through a year long "Incompetion" (incubation and competition) platform. This program sparked and created awareness about the impact of pro-poor technologies via direct seminars, media coverage, and target specific promotional campaigns while engaging more than 3,000 young innovators.

What we offer

Trainings

Hard work pays off. Contestants get extensive soft skill, business and technical trainings in order to ameliorate their projects. In addition, they are trained to deliver effective strategic pitch to funders or partners.

Mentorships

A unique and highly regarded component of Solve IT is our mentoring service. Each contestant has the opportunity to work with a mentor that provides one-on-one, and in-depth guidance throughout the competition and follow-up on the growth of their projects.

Exposure

Get new friends and make new memories. Our competitors get to meet people from all over the country, in different fields, each with their own dreams.

Incubation

Think Big, Start Small, Scale Fast! To help them create successful startups, contestants are provided with a systemized and comprehensive range of support, including expertise assistance, business support services, and vast networking opportunities.

Investor connections

Grow your connections, Our platform enables contestants to meet and present their projects to investors both on national and international grounds.

Boot camp

Brush ups before the big day! Aside from Intensive trainings, contestants get effective assistance on their week spots and work on their projects to make it the best it could possibly be.
